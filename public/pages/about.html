About

How We Began...

Terminus Industries began in 2016 as the brainchild of 3 teens. The initial plans were decidedly broader, between custom Transformers designs, Minecraft mods, and even an animation project meant to bridge Tokyo Ghoul and Transformers (with some cheeky Undertale and Deathnote references, too!). Obviously, much of this fell through, and as time progressed members came and left, including one of the founding members, after a major internal conflict.

Today, Terminus' scope is a bit narrower, with a focus on the following:

- The aforementioned Transformers designs, nested under Nightstrike's Customs.

- Minecraft modding and datapacks.

- Web development (templates for digital stuff, and some APIs for Java/JS)

- Music composition

...As well as a few minor things, including some niche community projects.

What Is Terminus Industries?

Terminus Industries primarily is an outlet for Nightstrike's Customs, a branch dedicated to CAD and 3D printing, with a bias towards Transformers. Other projects include music production and coding ventures.
